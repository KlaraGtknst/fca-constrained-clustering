\section{Method}
\label{sec:method}

Our approach integrates \ac{fca} with topic modeling to generate hierarchical document clusters guided by \ac{mlb} constraints. 
The core of our methodology relies on the insight that both the structural constraints provided by expert knowledge (\autoref{sec:constrained_latticed}) and the statistical patterns derived from topic models (\autoref{sec:topic_model}) can be formalized as closure operators. 
By computing the intersection of their respective closure systems (\autoref{sec:integrated_lattice_computation}), we derive a concept lattice that respects both logical constraints and empirical data.
