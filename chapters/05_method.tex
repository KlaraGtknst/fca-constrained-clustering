\section{Method}
\label{sec:method}

Our approach integrates \ac{fca} with external knowledge to construct concept lattices.
These lattices can in turn be translated into hierarchical document clusters (\autoref{sec:formal_context_to_hierarchical_cluster}) guided by the respective external knowledge source.

We consider two types of external knowledge bases for our case study:
(i) constraints derived from a gold-label topic hierarchy (\autoref{sec:computation}), and
(ii) topic hierarchy obtained from a topic model (\autoref{sec:topic_model}).
