\section{Method}
\label{sec:method}

Our approach integrates \ac{fca} with external knowledge to construct concept lattices.
These lattices can in turn be translated into hierarchical document clusters guided by the respective external knowledge source.

We consider two types of external knowledge bases for our case study:
(i) constraints derived from a gold-label topic hierarchy (\autoref{sec:gt_mlb}), and
(ii) topic hierarchy obtained from a topic model (\autoref{sec:topic_model}).
